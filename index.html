Our training

program

strength &

balance

Strength training will make your muscles stronger, allowing you to make more rapid and explosive movements to improve punching power. Balance keeps us standing after taking a hit or pivoting on the balls of our feet. It takes a stable, centered stance to land a punch and a lot of practice.

Get in touch

speed, Agility &

reaction

The faster you are, the better your chances to land punches that your opponent will not see coming. When those punches land, they do the most damage. Training for agility increases our ability to stop, start and change directions with ease and speed. Next, reaction time is very important if you want to be a better boxer. Improve your overall hand-eye-foot coordination.

muscular endurance &

anaerobic fitness

With training, your muscles can keep contracting for longer durations without getting tired, allowing you to train harder for longer and burn more calories. Anaerobic conditioning is a key part of being successful in boxing. With this, the boxer is able to hold out as much as possible.
